¿Qué pasa si ignoro el problema con la llave o ignición? What happens if I ignore the key or ignition problem? ▾

It generally gets worse. A small problem like a stiff key can end with the ignition completely blocked, leaving you stranded. It's better to address it before it fails completely.
¿Por qué mi Nissan/Hyundai/Kia dice "Key not detected"? Why does my Nissan/Hyundai/Kia say "Key not detected"? ▾
Generalmente es por batería baja en la llave o problema de señal. Prueba acercando la llave directamente al botón de encendido. Si persiste, llámanos. Generally it's due to a low key battery or signal problem. Try bringing the key directly to the start button. If it persists, call us.
¿Puedo arrancar el carro si la batería de la llave está muerta? Can I start the car if the key battery is dead? ▾

Yes — in many modern vehicles you can bring the key directly to the start button. The car can detect it via internal antenna even with a low battery.
